Welcome to Our Research Lab

The MDID Research Lab focuses on studying metabolic disorders and infectious diseases in animal models. We combine field studies, molecular biology, and computational approaches to advance knowledge in zoology, pharmacology, and human health. Our lab integrates traditional experimental research with cutting-edge in silico and translational methods.

Explore Our Research

Our Focus Areas

  • Anti-HIV Drug Discovery using computational and experimental methods
  • Metabolic Disorders: Anti-diabetic and anti-obesity bioactive compounds
  • Computational Biology: Virtual screening, molecular docking, QSAR, MD simulations
  • Phytochemistry & Ethnobotany: Therapeutic compounds from medicinal plants
  • Antimicrobial Drug Resistance: Studying bacterial and fungal resistance mechanisms
